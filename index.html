What is Lightspeed Squarespace Integration?

At its core, Lightspeed Squarespace integration is a technology bridge created by SKUPlugs. Think of it as a translator that allows both platforms to understand and exchange information.

Lightspeed is known for its robust retail POS and management features: handling payments, tracking inventory, generating reports, and managing staff. Squarespace, on the other hand, is widely used for building elegant websites and online stores with customizable templates and powerful e-commerce features.

How it Works:

  • A product added in Lightspeed automatically appears in Squarespace with the same description, price, and image.
  • When an item sells online, inventory in Lightspeed decreases instantly.
  • Customer information is captured and shared across both platforms.
  • Online orders flow directly into Lightspeed for smooth processing.
